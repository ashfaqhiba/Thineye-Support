Thin Eye

Visual search, simplified.

Thin Eye is a modern visual search app that allows you to explore the internet using images instead of text. Simply upload or capture a photo, and discover visually similar content from across the web.

What We Focus On

  • Simplicity — no accounts, no friction
  • Speed — fast and responsive results
  • Privacy — minimal data handling
  • Accessibility — built for everyone

Our Vision

We believe searching should feel natural. Thin Eye is built to make visual discovery effortless, turning everyday images into meaningful insights.

Privacy First

  • No account required
  • No personal data collection
  • No tracking or profiling
  • Temporary image processing only
