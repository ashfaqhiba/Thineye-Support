Privacy Notice

Effective Date: April 2026

Thin Eye is built to deliver visual search while keeping data handling simple and respectful. This page explains what is processed when you use the app and why.

1. No Account Needed

You can use Thin Eye without creating an account. We do not ask for or store personal identifiers such as your name, email, phone number, or government ID.

2. What You Provide

  • Images: photos you choose from your device or capture with the camera.
  • Search Actions: requests you trigger to find visually similar images.

This information is used only to complete your request. It is not used for ads, profiling, or behavioral tracking.

3. Where Results Come From

The app compares your image with content available on publicly accessible sources across the internet. It does not access private, restricted, or paid services. Results may link to third-party websites that we do not control.

4. How Images Are Processed

  • Images are transmitted using encrypted connections.
  • Processing may involve trusted external infrastructure.
  • Images are used only to generate your search results.

Images are not used for identity recognition, advertising, or training machine learning models.

5. Retention

We keep data only as long as needed to complete your request. Images are processed temporarily and removed shortly after completion. We do not keep a permanent copy on our servers.

Any optional history remains on your device and can be cleared at any time.

6. Permissions

The app may request access to your camera and photo library so you can capture or select images. You can allow or revoke these permissions in your device settings at any time.

7. Technical Data

To keep the app stable, we may collect limited technical information such as device type, OS version, and crash logs. This data does not include your images and is not linked to your identity.

8. Notifications

We may send notifications related to app functionality or important updates. You can disable notifications at any time in your device settings.

9. Payments

If you choose to purchase premium features, payments are processed by Apple. We do not receive or store your payment details—only your subscription status.

10. Your Choices

  • Remove images from your device
  • Clear app data
  • Uninstall the app

Once removed, the data is no longer accessible to us.

11. Children

Thin Eye is not intended for children under 13. If we become aware of data from a child, it will be deleted.

12. Security

We apply reasonable safeguards to protect data during transmission and processing. No system is completely secure, but we continually work to improve protections.

13. Updates

This Privacy Notice may change over time. Updates will be reflected by a new effective date. Continued use of the app means you accept the updated version.
